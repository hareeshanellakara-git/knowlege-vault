About Knowledge Vault

Project Introduction

Knowledge Vault is a modern student productivity platform designed to centralize academic resources, track exam preparation, and monitor study progress in real time. It provides a structured digital workspace where students can manage subjects, visualize performance analytics, and stay consistent through smart tracking features.

Our Mission

To simplify academic planning by offering a unified platform that helps students organize study materials, track progress efficiently, and build consistent learning habits through intelligent tracking systems.

Our Vision

To evolve into a comprehensive digital academic assistant that leverages real-time data, analytics, and smart features to enhance student productivity and learning outcomes on a global scale.

Key Features

Exam Tracker

Manage subjects, exam dates, and preparation progress efficiently.

Study Streak

Build consistent study habits with automatic daily streak tracking.

Smart Dashboard

Visualize progress, upcoming exams, and performance insights in real time.

Upload Notes

Organize and access study materials in one centralized space.

Bookmarks

Save and revisit useful learning resources anytime.

Exam Countdown

Stay aware of upcoming exams with dynamic countdown tracking.
