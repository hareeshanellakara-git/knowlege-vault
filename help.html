Help & User Guide

How to Use Knowledge Vault

  1. Navigate to the Exam Tracker and add your subjects along with exam dates.
  2. Update your preparation by marking subjects as Completed or Revised.
  3. Maintain consistency and build your study streak automatically.
  4. Monitor your progress, insights, and upcoming exams on the Dashboard.
  5. Organize your study materials using Upload Notes and Bookmarks.

Feature Guidance

  • Track subjects efficiently with smart countdowns to exam dates.
  • Dashboard provides real-time insights, activity tracking, and focus recommendations.
  • Study streak feature motivates consistent daily learning habits.
  • Upload and Bookmark sections help centralize all study resources.
  • Data is seamlessly synchronized across the application for a smooth experience.
